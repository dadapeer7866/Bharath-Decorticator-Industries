About Bharath Decorticator Industries

Bharath Decorticator Industries, established in 2004 and situated in Hiriyur, Karnataka, is recognized as one of the top groundnut decorticator manufacturers in India. Our facility is equipped to fabricate exceptional decorticator machinery that stands out for its efficiency and resilience.

Featuring an impressive array of products like Single Deckar, Double Deckar, Triple Deckar, Elevators, and D-stoners, we reflect the zenith of manufacturing excellence. We craft these sophisticated machines to perform with precision, catering to the global standards of the agricultural industry.

Our company offers complete turnkey solutions complemented by tailored engineering services, ensuring that every client benefits from our advanced groundnut decortication machinery. The expansive installation of our equipment across India is a testament to our dedication to quality and customer satisfaction.

Guided by a team of seasoned technocrats, Bharath Decorticator Industries is propelled by an ethos of innovation and an unyielding commitment to excellence. Our workforce, comprising skilled and passionate professionals, is the backbone of our progressive growth and enhanced production capabilities.

Choose Bharath Decorticator Industries for unparalleled groundnut decortication solutions. Get in touch with us to empower your operations with leading-edge machinery designed for peak performance.

Our Services

Bharath Decorticator Industries leads the way in the advanced engineering of groundnut decorticator machinery, ensuring an unparalleled transformation in the processing capabilities of businesses across the globe. Our automatic decorticators, perfected for high-performance (HPS) mill plants, stand out for their impeccable precision, delivering flawless groundnuts free from skin and seed faults.

Embracing innovation at every turn, our decorticators are designed with the highest standards of efficiency in mind, boasting an impressive processing capacity of up to 150 bags per hour. Each machine is a product of intensive research and collaborative development, reflecting our dedication to elevating the quality and quantity of your nut processing needs.
