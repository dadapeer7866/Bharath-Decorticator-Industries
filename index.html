About Us

Established in the year 2004, Bharath Decorticator Industries is a proud manufacturer and exporter of high-quality groundnut decorticator machinery. Our Services

At Bharath Decorticator Industries, we specialize in the manufacture of innovative groundnut decorticator machinery capable of transforming the efficiency of your operations. Our machinery is designed for the hand-picked and selected (HPS) mill plants, with a focus on delivering high-quality nuts without skin or seed faults. Our cutting-edge decorticators are automatic and boast a capacity of 100-150 bags per hour. Discover how our machines can enhance your productivity and output.
