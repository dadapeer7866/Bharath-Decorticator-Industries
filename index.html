About Bharath Decorticator Industries

Bharath Decorticator Industries, founded in 2004 in Hiriyur, Karnataka, stands out as a top manufacturer of groundnut decorticator machines and peanut decorticator machines in India. Our facility is designed to create machines known for their efficiency and strength.

We offer a range of products including Single Deckar, Double Deckar, Triple Deckar, Elevators, and D-stoners, showcasing our manufacturing excellence. We build these advanced machines to meet global agricultural standards.

We provide complete turnkey solutions with custom engineering services, ensuring all clients receive our top-notch groundnut processing machinery. Our widespread installations across India highlight our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.

Led by a team of experienced experts, Bharath Decorticator Industries thrives on innovation and a strong commitment to excellence. Our dedicated and skilled team drives our growth and enhances our production capabilities.
